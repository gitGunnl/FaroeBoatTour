Fishing Trip to Hvítanesi

Experience the magic of the Faroe Islands on the fishing trip to Hvítanes. Aboard Thomasia, our traditional Faroese boat, you’ll embark on a captivating 2 or 4-hour journey filled with history, natural beauty, and the excitement of fishing.

Trip Highlights:

  • Historical Landmarks: Set sail from Havnini and cruise past the historic Skansin fort, built in 1580 by the legendary Magnus Heinason to ward off pirates. Relive the captivating tales of Faroese bravery and adventure.
  • Scenic Beauty: Navigate the tranquil fjords, gliding between the majestic Hoyvíkshólmi and Hoyvíksfles. Immerse yourself in the stunning views of Hoyvíkina and the untouched landscapes, showcasing the Faroe Islands' natural splendor.
  • Authentic Fishing Experience: Engage in traditional Faroese fishing with both ancient hand reels and modern equipment. Our top-quality gear offers you the chance to catch cod, haddock, whiting, and more. Experience the thrill of the catch and connect with a centuries-old tradition.
  • Peaceful Ambiance: With the engine off, embrace the serene sounds of nature. The gentle lapping of waves and melodious seabird songs create a peaceful atmosphere, perfect for relaxation and reflection.
  • Comfort and Safety: Enjoy peace of mind with all necessary safety equipment onboard, including life jackets and rescue boats. Relax with complimentary tea, coffee, and sweet treats as you sail on our well-maintained vessel.
  • Catch of the Day: Take home your fresh catch, a delicious memento of your Faroese fishing adventure.

Book your fishing trip to Hvítanesi today and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Fishing Trip to Tangafjørð

Embark on an unforgettable adventure with our fishing trip to Tangafjørð fishing trip. Aboard Thomasia, our traditional Faroese boat, you'll dive into a world of history, natural beauty, and the excitement of fishing. Choose between a 2 or 4-hour journey and explore the wonders of the Faroe Islands like never before.

Trip Highlights:

  • Breathtaking Scenic Voyage: Depart from the tranquil Kaldbaksbotn and sail through the picturesque Kaldbaksfjord. Keep an eye out for playful whales, especially porpoises, as we navigate these rich, biodiverse waters.
  • Rich Historical Journey: Pass by Sund, where visionary Hans Mortensen once dreamed of grand industrial enterprises. Further along, glide past Kalbak, home to the Faroe Islands' first fiberglass boat factory. Discover the fascinating history of these traditionally designed boats, many of which are still in use today.
  • Authentic Fishing Experience: Anchor under Síðu in Tangafjørð, between Kaldbak and Kollafjørður, to fish in abundant waters. Catch cod, haddock, saithe, and whiting using both ancient hand reels and modern equipment. Experience authentic Faroese fishing with lines that have been used for centuries.
  • Peaceful Natural Ambiance: With the engine off, embrace the tranquility of nature. The gentle lapping of waves and melodious seabird songs create a serene atmosphere, perfect for relaxation and reflection.
  • Comfort and Safety: Rest assured with all necessary safety equipment on board, including life jackets and rescue boats. Our well-maintained vessel ensures a safe and comfortable journey for up to 8 passengers plus the captain.
  • Catch of the Day: Take home your fresh catch, a delicious memento of your Faroese fishing adventure.
  • Complimentary Refreshments: Enjoy complimentary tea, coffee, and a sweet treat as we sail through the scenic fjords and open waters.

Book Your Adventure: This exclusive Faroese experience is not to be missed.

Fishing Trip to Nólsoyarfirði

Embark on an unforgettable fishing adventure in Nólsoyarfirði aboard our traditional Faroese boat, Thomasia. This unique trip blends rich history, natural beauty, and the thrill of fishing in the pristine waters of the Faroe Islands.

Trip Highlights:

  • Historical and Scenic Route: Depart from Tórshavn and sail past the picturesque Argjaland and the impressive Glyvursnes, each with its own fascinating stories. Marvel at the expansive fish farms where valuable salmon swim, contributing to the Faroe Islands' vibrant economy.
  • Authentic Faroese Fishing Experience: Try your hand at traditional Faroese fishing with high-quality gear, including hand reels with lines used for centuries by locals. Experience the unique combination of ancient and modern fishing techniques to catch fish like cod, haddock, and whiting.
  • Serene Natural Beauty: Navigate towards the beautiful Borðan and its lighthouse, depending on weather and currents. With the engine off, enjoy the tranquil sounds of nature—the gentle lapping of waves and the melodious songs of seabirds in the soft breeze.
  • Comfort and Safety: Relax knowing our boat is equipped with all necessary safety features, including life jackets and rescue boats. Enjoy complimentary tea, coffee, and sweet treats as you sail on our well-maintained vessel.
  • Catch of the Day: Take home your fresh catch, a delicious memento of your Faroese fishing adventure.

Why Choose Us:

  • Personalized and Intimate Experience: With small group sizes, we ensure personalized attention and insights from our knowledgeable captain, making your experience intimate and enriching.
  • Exclusive Access: Explore unique fishing spots and scenic locations, offering an exclusive perspective of the Faroe Islands.

Trip to Sundalagið

Experience the beauty and history of Sundalagið with Captain Grækaris aboard the traditional Faroese boat, Thomasia. This trip offers a unique blend of natural beauty, historical insights, and the tranquility of the longest sound in the Faroe Islands.

Trip Highlights:

  • Historical and Scenic Route: Sail through the serene waters between Eysturoy and Streymoy, protected by mountains on both sides. Hear the fascinating history of the area, including the best-preserved whaling station at Áir.
  • Authentic Faroese Fishing Experience: Try traditional Faroese fishing with hand reels and lines used for centuries. Catch fish like cod and haddock while enjoying the calm waters.
  • Rich Industrial History: Learn about the whaling industry that sustained the people of Sundalagið in the 1900s, including stories from Captain Grækaris about his grandfather who worked at the Áir whaling station.
  • Stunning Marine Life: Discover the rich marine life under the keel of Thomasia, including lobsters, sea urchins, and various types of crabs, which are delicacies on luxury tables worldwide.
  • Comfort and Safety: Enjoy complimentary tea, coffee, and refreshments. Our boat is equipped with all necessary safety features, ensuring a comfortable and safe journey.

Why Choose Us:

  • Exclusive Access: Explore unique scenic locations and fishing spots, offering an exclusive perspective of the Faroe Islands.
