What to Expect on Our Boat Tours

Embark on an extraordinary journey through the stunning waters of the Faroe Islands with our friendly and knowledgeable captain, Grækaris Magnussen. Our tours offer more than just sightseeing – they provide a deep dive into the rich history, vibrant culture, and breathtaking landscapes of this unique archipelago.
Captivating Stories: Hear fascinating tales and local legends from Captain Grækaris, whose deep connection to the islands and engaging storytelling will leave you enthralled.
Scenic Routes: Enjoy the serene beauty of the Faroe Islands' cliffs, fjords, and wildlife as we navigate calm, pristine waters.
Personalized Experience: Our small group sizes ensure a personalized and intimate experience, allowing you to ask questions and learn more about the islands.
Comfort and Safety: Relax and take in the views with the peace of mind that comes from our well-maintained boat and experienced crew.
Exclusive Stops: Explore historic sites like the Áir whaling station, where you can step ashore and discover well-preserved structures and captivating stories from the past.
Join us for an adventure that combines the thrill of exploration with the comfort of a guided tour, making your visit to the Faroe Islands truly unforgettable.
